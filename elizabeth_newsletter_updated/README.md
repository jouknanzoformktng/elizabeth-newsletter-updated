# Elizabeth Jeanty - Love Where You Live Newsletter

A high-end, culturally relevant Valentine's Day newsletter for South Florida real estate professional Elizabeth Jeanty.

## Features

- **Lavender & Gold Theme**: Sophisticated color palette with rich purples and luxury gold accents
- **Interactive Neighborhood Generator**: Engaging form for personalized market snapshots
- **Cultural Focus**: Celebrates Caribbean & Haitian-American heritage
- **Market Insights**: February Market Pulse section with key data
- **South Florida Lifestyle**: Highlights the unique benefits of living in the area

## Sections

1. Hero with "Love Where You Live" theme
2. About Elizabeth Jeanty
3. Neighborhood Spotlight (North Miami & Fort Lauderdale)
4. Inspirational Quote
5. Featured Property Showcase
6. Interactive Neighborhood & Lifestyle Snapshot Generator
7. South Florida Lifestyle
8. February Market Pulse
9. Culture & Community
10. Contact Footer

## Technologies

- HTML5
- CSS3 (Custom properties, Grid, Flexbox)
- Google Fonts (Playfair Display, Montserrat)
- Font Awesome Icons
